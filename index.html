About Campus Lost & Found

Our platform makes it simple for students and staff to report lost or found items on campus. Whether it’s a misplaced phone, a forgotten book, or a lost ID card, we help reconnect belongings with their rightful owners.

1

Report an Item

Quickly report lost or found items with essential details such as description, date, and location.

2

Browse Listings

Explore reported items easily using categories and filters to quickly find what you’re looking for.

3

Reconnect

Connect with the rightful owner or finder to return the item securely and hassle-free.
